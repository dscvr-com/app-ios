Optonaut lets you capture and share unique moments in a completely
new and immersive way. Record virtual reality panorama pictures in 3D just using your smartphone. You can view them with VR glasses such as a Google Cardboard.

Bring your loved ones with you on your journeys and let them see the world through your eyes. Explore beautiful new places and refresh your memories of past experiences.

* Record VR photographs using your phone
* Works with most VR glasses including Google Cardboard, Zeiss VRone and others
* Discover unique places you've never seen before
* Join the community to follow your friends and meet new interesting people

Note: The community is invite-only at the current stage. You can request an invite on our website.
